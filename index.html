Projects

DigiDocs

The project has been built in Android using Kotlin. The main goal of this project is to provide the nominee with access to the user's legal documents such as property papers, ect . This addresses a significant issue where individuals who die accidentally leave behind property and documents that their family members even are not aware about. DigiDocs provides a platform where users can store legal documents, and the rightful heirs can access all necessary documents easily with the permission of the owner or the user.
